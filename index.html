Bottlenose dolphin

Dolphins is one of the more intelligent species of the planet. It can living in all the oceans around the world.

  • Scientific Name: Tursiops truncatus.
  • Average Length: 2-4 meter
  • Average Lifespan:40-60 years
  • Habitat: Ocean.

It's one of the most beautiful animals in the planet. It travel in big groups and cooperate each with others to fishing.
